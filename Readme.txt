RSS feed content horizontal scroller on your site.

Usage:

**************Show scroller using php code*********************

<?php rsslshowrsscroller('feed url', 'width of scroller', 'height of scroller', 'scrolling speed');>
Example: <?php rsslshowrsscroller("http://au.rss.news.yahoo.com/top_stories.xml",'400','20','2'); ?>

**************Show scroller using shortcode in content*********************

[rsscroller url=feed url width=width of scroller height=height of scroller speed=scrolling speed]
Example: [rsscroller url=http://au.rss.news.yahoo.com/top_stories.xml width=400 height=20 speed=2]
